Features

Sprite offers many time saving, easy to use, features to keep you developing. Here are a few:

Pick your language

Just staring out? You can use Ruby to get most of your game up and running. Need more power? Objective-C (and just plain C) is under the hood for you to use. At the end of the process, everything get's transformed into C for raw power.

Consolidated Testing

Need to test your game? Of course you do. You can run your game directly in the app to get a sense of how it plays immediately. We think it's simply the best way to create quiality games.

Integrated Graphics Editor

What game would be complete without graphics? Unless you're creating the next Zork great grapics are paramount. We've provided an editor for keyframe animation, scene creation, paralax scrolling scenes and more.
